CWA / SCA CANADA

  • Print Design

Communications Workers of America  (CWA / SCA) Canada is the oldest and only all-media union in Canada, with about 6,000 members at 17 Locals across the country. They are an impressive organization doing critical work in representing workers in the communications industry.  

In designing the print material for their events and campaigns for the past three years, we came up with material that reflected the political stance of the organization while utilizing playful, detailed, and informative graphics.
