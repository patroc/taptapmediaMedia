TORONTO PALESTINE FILM FESTIVAL

  • Print Design
  • Web Design
  • Festival Trailer

The Toronto Palestine Film Festival (TPFF) is a volunteer-run, non-profit organization dedicated to bringing Palestinian cinema, music, cuisine and art to GTA audiences.

For the past 3 years TPFF has commissioned us to come up with a brand new design and look for the annual festival. As with any non-profit festival, TPFF reaches out to larger and more diverse audiences every year.

They want each year's "look" to resonate both with people who are familiar with Palestinian film, art, and music, but also those who may not be. As a result, the festival has grown and evolved, and each year the design becomes an exciting and challenging project.
